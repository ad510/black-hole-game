Death by Time Travel

If you approach a time field, you will time travel into the future.
But if you get too close, it will take you to the end of the universe.
When that happens, it's game over.


Time fields are visible. Asteroids have no effect on gameplay.


Time fields are invisible on a black background, so you must find them by studying their time manipulation effects on asteroids and rocket exhaust.
Use arrow keys to move. Avoid the time fields!
